# Acme Corp Benefits Guide

**Plan Year:** January 1 -- December 31, 2024
**Last Updated:** November 1, 2024
**Document Owner:** People Operations -- Total Rewards

## Overview

Acme Corp provides a comprehensive benefits package supporting the health, financial security, and well-being of our employees and their families. All benefits are effective on your first day of employment unless otherwise noted.

## Health Insurance

### Medical
Acme Corp offers three medical plan options through Anthem Blue Cross:

| Plan | Employee Premium | Employee + Family | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|------------|-------------------|
| **PPO Gold** | $0 | $250/mo | $500 / $1,000 | $3,000 / $6,000 |
| **PPO Silver** | $0 | $150/mo | $1,500 / $3,000 | $5,000 / $10,000 |
| **HDHP + HSA** | $0 | $75/mo | $2,800 / $5,600 | $5,500 / $11,000 |

Acme Corp covers 100% of employee premiums. The HDHP includes an employer HSA contribution of $1,000 (individual) or $2,000 (family) annually.

### Dental
Delta Dental PPO: 100% preventive, 80% basic, 50% major procedures. $2,500 annual max per person. Orthodontia up to $2,000 lifetime for dependents under 26.

### Vision
VSP Vision: annual eye exams (100% after $10 copay), $200 frame allowance, $150 contact lens allowance. 15% laser vision correction discount through participating providers.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

Administered through Fidelity with immediate eligibility and full vesting:
- **Company match:** 100% of first 4% of salary, plus 50% of next 2% (max 5% employer contribution).
- **Contribution limits:** Per IRS limits ($23,000 for 2024; $30,500 for age 50+).
- **Options:** Target-date funds, index funds, self-directed brokerage, traditional pre-tax and Roth after-tax.
- **Auto-enrollment:** 6% for new hires. Adjust anytime via Fidelity NetBenefits.

## Life and Disability Insurance

- **Basic life:** 2x annual salary (up to $500,000), fully employer-paid.
- **Supplemental life:** Available at group rates up to 5x salary. Evidence of insurability required above 3x.
- **Short-term disability:** 60% of base salary for up to 26 weeks. 14-day elimination period. Employer-paid.
- **Long-term disability:** 60% of base salary after STD exhaustion, up to age 65. Employer-paid.

## Wellness Programs

- **Wellness Stipend:** $100/month for gym, fitness classes, meditation apps. Reimbursed via Expensify.
- **Mental Health:** Lyra Health with 12 therapy/coaching sessions per year for employees and dependents.
- **Ergonomics:** $500 home office stipend (one-time, upon hire). In-office assessments available.
- **EAP:** Confidential counseling, legal, and financial planning through ComPsych (24/7).
- **Wellness Days:** Two additional paid days per year, separate from PTO and sick leave.

## Commuter Benefits

Pre-tax via WageWorks: up to $315/month for transit, $315/month for parking. $50/month bike commuting reimbursement. San Francisco office employees receive a fully subsidized Clipper card.

## Enrollment Periods

| Event | Window |
|-------|--------|
| **New hire** | Within 30 days of start date |
| **Open enrollment** | November 1--30 (effective January 1) |
| **Qualifying life event** | Within 30 days (marriage, birth, adoption, divorce, loss of coverage) |

## Additional Perks

- **Meals:** $25/day credit when in-office (Grubhub for Business).
- **Cell phone:** $75/month stipend. **Sabbatical:** 4 weeks paid after 5 years.
- **Learning:** $3,000/year professional development budget (see Employee Handbook, Section 6).
