# Acme Corp New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Last Updated:** October 10, 2024
**Document Owner:** People Operations
**Audience:** New Hires, Managers, Onboarding Buddies

## Pre-Start (Before Day 1)

- [ ] Sign offer letter and employment agreements via DocuSign.
- [ ] Complete background check authorization (Checkr).
- [ ] Submit new hire paperwork: W-4, I-9, direct deposit, emergency contact.
- [ ] Select benefits elections in Workday (medical, dental, vision, 401k).
- [ ] Review and acknowledge the Employee Handbook and Information Security Policy.

## Day 1: Welcome and Setup

Your first day begins at 10:00 AM. Report to the front desk or join the virtual Welcome Session if remote.

### IT Setup
- [ ] Receive company laptop (MacBook Pro or ThinkPad, pre-configured by IT).
- [ ] Set up corporate SSO (Okta) and enroll in MFA (hardware key provided).
- [ ] Configure email (Google Workspace), Slack, and calendar.
- [ ] Install required software: CrowdStrike EDR, Tailscale VPN, 1Password, Zoom.
- [ ] Verify access to core systems: GitHub, Jira, Confluence, Figma (role-dependent).

### Facilities and Orientation
- [ ] Receive building badge and parking pass (in-office employees). Complete office tour.
- [ ] Attend Welcome Session with People Ops (company history, values, org structure).
- [ ] Meet your manager for 1:1 introduction and initial expectations discussion.
- [ ] Meet your onboarding buddy (assigned by People Ops; same or adjacent team).
- [ ] Team lunch or virtual coffee with immediate team members.

## Week 1: Foundation

### Required Training (complete by end of Week 1)
- [ ] Security Awareness Training (30 min, KnowBe4).
- [ ] Harassment Prevention Training (1 hr, Ethena).
- [ ] Data Privacy and GDPR Fundamentals (45 min, internal LMS).
- [ ] Acme Corp Product Overview (1 hr, recorded session on Confluence).

### Systems and People
- [ ] Complete Jira onboarding project (pre-populated tasks for your role).
- [ ] Review team wiki and documentation in Confluence.
- [ ] Set up local development environment (engineering roles -- see Engineering Onboarding Guide).
- [ ] Gain access to relevant Slack channels (your buddy will provide a list).
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with each direct team member (15-30 min each).
- [ ] Attend your first team standup or sync meeting.
- [ ] Post introduction in #new-hires Slack channel (People Ops provides a template).

## First Month: Integration

- [ ] Shadow at least 3 customer calls or demos (customer-facing roles).
- [ ] Complete role-specific training modules assigned in the LMS.
- [ ] Read the last two quarterly business reviews (QBRs) on Confluence.
- [ ] Review current quarter OKRs for your team and department.
- [ ] Complete your first meaningful contribution (first PR, design review, sales call, etc.).
- [ ] Participate in at least one cross-functional meeting or project.
- [ ] Attend a company All-Hands meeting (first Thursday of each month).
- [ ] Week 2 check-in with manager (structured feedback conversation).
- [ ] Week 4 check-in with manager (progress review, address blockers).
- [ ] Provide onboarding feedback via the New Hire Survey (sent at Day 30).

## 90-Day Milestones

- [ ] Collaborate with manager to set quarterly OKRs or goals in Lattice.
- [ ] Demonstrate independent ownership of at least one workstream or project.
- [ ] Complete introductory 1:1s with key stakeholders outside your immediate team.
- [ ] Identify a mentor (formal or informal) for ongoing career development.
- [ ] Attend at least one social event, ERG meeting, or interest group gathering.
- [ ] Understand and articulate Acme Corp's values and how they apply to your work.

### Manager Responsibilities at 90 Days
- [ ] Conduct a formal 90-day review conversation.
- [ ] Confirm role expectations and success metrics for the remainder of the year.
- [ ] Close out the onboarding Jira project and transition to standard workflows.
